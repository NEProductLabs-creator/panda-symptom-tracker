Build a reusable screener wizard component for the PANS/PANDAS Companion 
app at components/ScreenerWizard.tsx (or .jsx, match the project).

The component must work in two modes controlled by a prop:
- mode="anonymous" (no auth, no database writes, sessionStorage only)
- mode="authenticated" (signed-in user, will be wired to Supabase in 
  the post-login prompt)

The component accepts:
- mode: "anonymous" | "authenticated"
- onComplete: (answers, resultBucket) => void
- initialAnswers (optional): for resuming or demo mode

UI requirements:
- Persistent yellow banner at the top of every step: warning icon + 
  "This is a screening tool, not a medical diagnosis. Only a licensed 
  healthcare provider can diagnose PANS or PANDAS."
- Progress bar showing current step out of 6
- Back and Next buttons. Back is disabled on step 1. Next is disabled 
  until the step's required fields are filled.
- Match the existing design system (typography, colors, button styles, 
  spacing).
- Mobile-first, responsive.

Six steps with the following questions:

Step 1: Onset
- "How old was your child when symptoms started?" (number input, 1-25)
- "Did the symptoms appear suddenly, within a few days?" (radio: Yes / 
  No / Not sure)
- "When did symptoms begin?" (date picker, optional, defaults to blank)

Step 2: Core symptoms (at least one required to proceed)
- "Has your child developed sudden OCD-like behaviors such as intrusive 
  thoughts, repeated rituals, or contamination fears?" (Yes / No)
  - If Yes, show optional textarea: "Briefly describe what you have 
    seen."
- "Has your child suddenly refused food or severely restricted eating?" 
  (Yes / No)
  - If Yes, show optional textarea: "Briefly describe."
- Validation: at least one must be Yes to proceed. If both No, show 
  helper text: "PANS and PANDAS diagnostic criteria require at least 
  one of these core symptoms. You can still complete the screener."

Step 3: Accompanying symptoms (checklist, multi-select)
Display as a clean grid of checkboxes with brief plain-language 
descriptions:
- Anxiety (new or sharply worsened)
- Emotional lability or depression
- Irritability, aggression, or oppositional behavior
- Behavioral or developmental regression (acting younger)
- School performance dropped suddenly
- Sensory issues (sound, light, touch sensitivity) or motor changes
- Sleep disturbance
- Urinary frequency or bedwetting (new)
- Handwriting changes
- Tics (motor or vocal)

- "In the weeks before symptoms started, did your child or anyone in 
  your household have any of the following?" (multi-select checkbox 
  list)
  - Strep throat (confirmed by test)
  - Sore throat
  - Scarlet fever
  - Sinus infection
  - Ear infection
  - Flu
  - COVID
  - Mycoplasma / walking pneumonia
  - Other illness (with textarea)
