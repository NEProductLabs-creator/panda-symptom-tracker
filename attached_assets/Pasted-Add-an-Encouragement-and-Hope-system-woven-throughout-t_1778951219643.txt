Add an Encouragement and Hope system woven throughout the app. This is not a separate page but a living presence across the entire experience.
Part 1: Daily affirmations on the home screen
Rotate a new message on the dashboard each day. Use this list as the starting set and add 5 more in the same tone:

"You showed up for your child today. That matters more than you know."
"PANS is treatable. Many children reach full remission. You are not alone in this fight."
"One hard day does not erase the progress you have made."
"The fact that you are tracking, advocating, and fighting for answers is extraordinary parenting."
"Other parents have walked this exact road and come out the other side. So will you."
"You did not cause this. You cannot always control it. But you are doing everything right."
"Recovery is not linear, but it is real, and it happens every day for PANS families."

Allow parents to tap a heart icon to save a favorite affirmation. Add a "Hope Board" screen where all saved affirmations are collected.
Part 2: Milestone celebrations
Trigger gentle celebration moments automatically when:

3 consecutive days have low or zero symptom scores: "3 good days in a row. Hold onto this."
A PTEC score drops significantly from the previous entry: "Your data shows real improvement. This is progress."
A parent completes and exports a doctor report: "You are your child's best advocate. This report could change everything."
The first week passes with no logged flare, first month, 3 months, and 6 months: celebrate each one with a milestone card

These celebrations should be soft and brief, not intrusive. A gentle banner or card that can be dismissed.
Part 3: Recovery stories panel
Add a small rotating panel on the Hope Board screen with 4 brief anonymized recovery stories. Write them in a warm, honest, parent voice. Example:
"Our daughter had rage episodes every day for 8 months. After finding the right antibiotic protocol, she went back to school full time. It took 14 months but she is mostly herself again."
Include a call to action at the bottom: "Add your story to help other families" that opens a simple email link.
Design: Use warm sunrise colors for this feature: soft amber, warm white, and pale gold. Avoid clinical blues and greens in this section specifically.
